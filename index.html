The Earth

Few facts about planet earth

Earth, the third planet from the Sun and our home, is unique for being the only known place in the universe to support life. It's a rocky, water-rich planet with a life-sustaining atmosphere, a magnetic field, and one natural satellite, the Moon. Formed about 4.5 billion years ago, it orbits the Sun and rotates on a tilted axis, which creates day, night, and seasons.

About The Earth

  • Distance from Sun: 149.6 million km
  • Radius: 6,378 km
  • Notable Moons: Moon

Top 3 Facts About Earth

  1. It is the only known planet to support life
  2. Approximately 71% of its surface is covered in water
  3. It is the third planet from the Sun, with an age of about 4.5 billion years
